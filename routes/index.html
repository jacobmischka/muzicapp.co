A social app to share and discover music with friends

And by friends, we mean all of your friends who are paying subscribers to Apple Music or Spotify Premium.

Music ain't free, folks.

Sick of your music?

How many times have we all felt “I’M SICK OF MY MUSIC”? The problem is when you need new music you have to go digging for it because you’re blasted with endless options in Spotify or Apple Music. Interesting enough our friends are sharing music on social media all the time but no one is discovering new music this way. This is because we all skip over those Instagram Stories of a Spotify screenshot with captions more or less like “love this song, must listen! fire emoji fire emoji.” Expecting your friends to listen to a song by sharing a song’s album cover is like posting a book cover and expecting your followers to go find and read the book’s summary. Not happening. So how do we make music sharing and discovery easier?

What if there was a social media for music but instead of sharing only album cover art, you could also create and share 30 second song previews. As you browse this music app, you can listen to your friends’ song previews and if you like what you hear, you can save the songs from this app to your Spotify or Apple Music library. Once that “I’m sick of my music” feeling comes along, you’ll already have an entire collection of new music you didn’t have to go digging for. This is MUZIC.
